KOTHA'S

We create environments where structural rigor meets artistic expression.

Founded by visionary architects, Kotha's Atelier represents the pinnacle of residential design, premium workspaces, and grand commercial spaces. Our design language is rooted in spatial harmony, utilizing premium raw materials like poured concrete, polished marble, patinated bronze, and warm structural timber.

We curate every detail, from the macro site planning to the micro custom furniture pieces, ensuring our clients receive a cohesive masterpiece that resonates with their distinct lifestyles.
